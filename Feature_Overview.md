# Vibe Crew App: Feature Overview

## Executive Summary
The Vibe Crew App is a comprehensive scheduling and organization solution designed specifically for Vibe Yoga. It transforms the manual, time-consuming process of managing Front Desk and Teaching shifts into a streamlined, automated, and accessible experience for both studio owners and employees. 

## The Challenge
Currently, scheduling relies on a manually maintained Google Sheet that lacks easy access, automated notifications, and self-service capabilities for employees. This leads to administrative overhead when handling shift changes, finding substitutes, and communicating updates.

## Key Capabilities & Business Value

### 1. Unified Multi-Platform Accessibility
Available on Android, iOS, macOS, and Windows desktop, ensuring all staff can access their schedules anytime, anywhere, on any device.

### 2. Intelligent Scheduling & Calendar
- **Comprehensive View:** A visual calendar displaying Month, Week, and Day views.
- **Advanced Filtering:** Easily filter by event type (shifts, vacations, workshops) or assigned employee.
- **Visual Clarity:** Assignable colors for different event types and clear calls-to-action for high-priority items like available shifts and important announcements.

### 3. Employee Self-Service & Shift Management
- **Shift Coverage:** Employees can request substitutes for their shifts directly within the app.
- **Shift Pickup:** Available shifts are broadcasted, allowing staff to easily sign up and fill gaps without back-and-forth texting.
- **Leave Management:** Simple entry of vacation and time-off requests.

### 4. Automated Notifications & Reminders
Keeps the entire team aligned with real-time push notifications for:
- New announcements and scheduling updates.
- Newly available shifts for pickup.
- Confirmations when a shift has been filled.
- Customizable reminders for upcoming scheduled shifts.

### 5. Seamless Calendar Integration
Integrates effortlessly with Apple and Google Calendars. The app automatically adds, updates, or removes shifts on the employee's personal calendar based on their current schedule in the app.

### 6. Role-Based Access Control
Secure and structured access tailored to your studio's hierarchy:
- **Owner:** Full system control, including administrator management.
- **Administrator:** Comprehensive scheduling and event management, plus request approvals.
- **Teacher & Front Desk:** Self-service shift management, vacation requests, and specialized event notifications.

### 7. Personalized User Experience
Modern, intuitive design featuring customizable themes, including standard Light/Dark modes, System default, and Vibe Yoga-inspired palettes (Olive, Sky, Grass, Mountains).

## Technology Foundation
Built for reliability and scale:
- **Client App:** Flutter (ensuring consistent performance across all platforms)
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Amazon Web Services (AWS) for secure, scalable server-side operations
